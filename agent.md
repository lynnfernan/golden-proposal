# Golden Foundation — 45-Day Revenue Recovery Pilot

## Client

- **Company:** Golden Limousine International (Ann Arbor, MI)
- **Prepared for:** Alina Verdiyan-Duval, Sean Duval, Joshua Zimdars, Sophie Rees, Kathryn DaCosta
- **Prepared by:** Lynn Fernando, Raphael Bennett, REV Global
- **Pilot start:** May 1, 2026

## Client's Stated Outcomes (exact words)

1. Quote to Booking Conversion
2. Recurring Volume
3. Systems that Support each other

## Proposal Summary

Revenue operations, AI orchestration, and an inside sales protocol built on the systems Golden already has (FreshSales, Dialpad, QuoteBot, Livery Coach). 17 deliverables across 45 days.
